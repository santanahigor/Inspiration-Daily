The Project

We all need motivation and encouragement from time to time, and if you don't need either of these at the moment, you certainly have friends or family who could benefit from both.
If you're looking for some motivational quotes or inspirational quotes to positively influence your day, you've come to the right place.
The main purpose of this project it's to help those people in need of a word of inspiration in a simple manner, just by clicking on a button and you will receive a phrase of inspiration for today.
